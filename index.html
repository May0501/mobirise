We often hear people in life, especially women, saying that if you want to be beautiful, you cannot be healthy. It is human nature to pursue beauty, but is this the truth? Next, please allow me to introduce my project to you-retro flower jewelry with the theme of traditional Chinese medicine health preserving.

MY PROJECT

项目介绍
Introduction

这是一款养生主题的花卉配饰。该配饰一系列共分为十二个月，每种代表了当月对应的时令花卉，采用金属镂空工艺；珠宝经过特制工艺，将花香和搭配的中药融入其中，使用户能在使用中通过气味来缓解疲劳。这是一款时尚与传统养生相结合的产品。 
This is a flower accessory with the theme of health preserving. The accessories are divided into 12 months in total, each of which represents the corresponding seasonal flowers of the current month and adopts metal hollowing-out craft; Jewelry is made through special craft, which integrates the fragrance of flowers and matched traditional Chinese medicine into it, it enables users to relieve fatigue through flavor during use. This is a combination of fashion and traditional health care products

用户调研
The Reserch

1

现象：现实生活中，很多人关注养生，但很少人实践,尤其是年轻人。

Phenomenon: in real life, many people pay attention to health preservation, but few people practice it, especially young people.

2

原因：传统的中医养生形式与现代生活格格不入，追求时尚美观的年轻人、尤其是年轻女性不会选择它们。

Reason: traditional Chinese medicine regimen forms are incompatible with modern life. Young people who pursue fashion and beauty, especially young women, will not choose them.  

3

设计：于是我从中医养生中常出现的香包入手，思考如何让现代物品具有养生功效。

Design: So I started with the sachet in traditional Chinese medicine health preserving, thinking about how to make modern goods have health preserving effect.
